NAKSHATRA

The Astronomy and Mathematics Society of NSUT

Nakshatra, the Astronomy and Mathematics Society of NSUT, is a student-run organization dedicated to exploring the wonders of the universe. Our mission is to foster a passion for astronomy and astrophotography among students and the community through engaging events, workshops, and stargazing sessions.
